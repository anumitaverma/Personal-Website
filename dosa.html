Dosa

DOSA on Fillmore is one of the two amazing India restaurants I will be advertising on my blog! When it comes to Indian food, I am peculiar with my food and DOSA has always satisfied my palates no matter what I have ordered! When I used to stay in San Francisco, my family and I went there once for lunch and ordered Dosa, what they are famous for. When I came back to San Francisco, I thought of going there once again to eat their DOSA. Turns out I ended up going there more than once! For those of you who follow me on SnapChat know that I have been DOSA probably A MILLION TIMES. Everything I have ordered thus far has been up to the mark, and I have loved every grain, every bite and every slurp of my food. In case you guys are wondering what I ordered:
